AARUUSH SRM

Aaruush is the student-run, official Annual Techno-Management Fest of SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Chennai. Team Aaruush is an ever-expanding family of talented individuals. Aaruush provides a platform to innovate a multitude of ideas which range fromMedical Sciences to Entrepreneurship, Robotics, and Game Development.
