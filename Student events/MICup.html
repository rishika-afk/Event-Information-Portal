MICROSOFT IMAGINE CUP

Microsoft Imagine Cup is conducted by Microsoft every year, and it has been two decades since they started. This program invites you to take on real-world problems submitted by IGOs, NGOs, and nonprofit organizations that you can then take on as part of your Imagine Cup project. The main idea behind this is “Code with purpose.” which assists in putting your ideas into action as you create solutions that have the potential to change the world. The winner of the Imagine cup will receive cash prizes, Azure Grant, and a mentoring session with Microsoft CEO.
